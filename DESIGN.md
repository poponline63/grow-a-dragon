# Grow a Dragon V2 - Game Design Document

## Vision
A polished, viral-quality Roblox dragon collecting game with high production value.
Think: Pet Simulator X meets Dragon City meets Adopt Me.

## Core Loop
1. **Earn Coins** - Click/tap to earn, complete quests, sell items
2. **Buy Eggs** - Visit the Egg Shop, choose from 6 tiers
3. **Hatch Dragons** - Place eggs on your plot, wait or speed up
4. **Raise Dragons** - Feed, play, train to level up
5. **Collect & Trade** - Build your collection, trade with other players
6. **Unlock Areas** - Progress unlocks new islands with rarer dragons

## World Layout
- **Spawn Island** - Central hub with dragon statue fountain, welcome arch
- **Egg Shop** - Beautiful building with egg displays on pedestals
- **Item Shop** - Potions, food, speed-ups
- **Quest Board** - Daily and weekly quests
- **Breeding Station** - Combine two dragons for new types
- **Player Plots** - Personal plot with incubator, dragon pen, food bowl
- **Fantasy Forest** (unlockable) - Nature/Shadow dragons
- **Crystal Caves** (unlockable) - Ice/Storm dragons
- **Sky Temple** (unlockable) - Light/Fire dragons

## Dragon System
- 6 Elements: Fire, Ice, Nature, Shadow, Light, Storm
- 6 Growth Stages: Baby → Juvenile → Teen → Adult → Elder → Legendary
- Stats: Strength, Speed, Magic (grow with level)
- Rarity: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythic
- 36 base dragon types (6 elements × 6 rarities)

## Egg System
- 6 egg tiers with increasing rarity chances
- Visual egg models that match rarity (stone → crystal → golden → flaming)
- Hatching animation with particle effects
- Speed-up with Robux or in-game items

## Monetization (Robux)
- Speed up hatching
- VIP egg (better odds)
- Extra plot slots
- Cosmetic dragon accessories
- Auto-collect gamepass

## UI Design
- Clean, modern UI with gradients and rounded corners
- Dragon inventory with card-style layout
- Rarity colors: Grey, Green, Blue, Purple, Gold, Red
- Smooth transitions and animations
- Mobile-friendly (large tap targets)

## Polish Features
- Particle effects on eggs (element-colored)
- Dragon idle animations (breathing, tail wagging)
- Smooth camera transitions
- Ambient music + SFX
- Loading screen with tips
- Daily login rewards
- Achievement system
