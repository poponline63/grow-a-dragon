# Grow a Dragon V3 — Pet Sim X × Grow a Garden

## Core Identity
"What if Pet Simulator X and Grow a Garden had a dragon baby?"
You hatch eggs, raise dragons on your personal plot, grow them through stages,
breed mutations, unlock new areas, and trade with other players.

## The Loop (30-second version)
1. Buy/find eggs → Place in incubator on your plot
2. Eggs hatch → Baby dragon appears in your pen
3. Feed & tend dragons → They grow through 6 stages (visible size changes)
4. Grown dragons unlock abilities → Send on expeditions for passive coins
5. Breed dragons → Discover element fusions (mutations!)
6. Unlock new areas → Find rarer eggs with exclusive dragons
7. Trade rare dragons → Flex your collection

## World Layout

### Spawn Hub (free)
- Giant dragon statue fountain (center)
- Egg Shop (NPC vendor with egg displays)
- Trading Plaza (player booths)
- Quest Board (daily/weekly)
- Leaderboards (rarest collection, total power, level)
- Portal to your plot

### Your Dragon Plot (free, upgradeable)
- **Incubators** (start with 1, buy up to 6)
- **Dragon Pen** (start small, upgrade 3 times for more capacity)
- **Food Bowl** (click to refill, or buy Auto-Feeder)
- **Breeding Altar** (unlocks at Level 8)
- **Expedition Board** (send adult dragons on timed missions)
- **Trophy Shelf** (display your rarest dragons)
- **Decorations** (unlockable cosmetic items)

### Enchanted Meadow (10K coins to unlock)
- Starter area, Common-Rare eggs
- Fire & Nature element eggs
- Easy quests, foraging spots

### Crystal Caverns (100K coins)
- Ice & Storm element eggs
- Glowing crystal environment
- Hidden rare egg spawns

### Shadow Realm (500K coins)
- Shadow & Light element eggs
- Dark aesthetic, floating islands
- Epic+ eggs only

### Sky Temple (2M coins)
- All elements, Legendary+ odds
- Cloud/heaven aesthetic
- "Huge" dragon eggs spawn here rarely

### Dragon's Peak (10M coins, endgame)
- Mythic eggs
- Boss dragons to fight
- Exclusive fusion recipes

## Dragon System

### Elements (6 base + fusions)
Base: Fire, Ice, Nature, Shadow, Light, Storm
Fusions (breeding combos):
- Fire + Ice = **Steam** 🌫️
- Fire + Nature = **Magma** 🌋
- Fire + Storm = **Plasma** ⚡
- Ice + Nature = **Frost Bloom** 🌸
- Ice + Shadow = **Void Ice** 🕳️
- Nature + Light = **Solar** ☀️
- Shadow + Light = **Eclipse** 🌑
- Shadow + Storm = **Thunder Dark** ⛈️
- Storm + Light = **Prism** 🌈
- Nature + Storm = **Tempest** 🌪️

### Growth Stages (6)
| Stage | Size | Time | Feeds Needed |
|-------|------|------|-------------|
| Baby | 3 studs | 0 min | 0 |
| Juvenile | 4.5 studs | 10 min | 5 |
| Teen | 6 studs | 30 min | 15 |
| Adult | 9 studs | 2 hours | 40 |
| Elder | 12 studs | 8 hours | 100 |
| Legendary | 16 studs | 24 hours | 250 |

### Rarity
Common (grey) → Uncommon (green) → Rare (blue) → Epic (purple) → Legendary (gold) → Mythic (red) → **HUGE** (rainbow, 0.01%)

### Dragon Stats
- **Power** (combat/expedition strength)
- **Speed** (expedition time reduction)
- **Luck** (better expedition rewards)
- Stats grow with each growth stage

### Enchanting
- Use **Dragon Essence** (earned from expeditions) to enchant dragons
- Enchants: +Power, +Speed, +Luck, +Shiny (visual glow), +Golden (recolor)
- **Shiny** dragons: 1/100 hatch chance, 2x base stats, sparkle effect
- **Golden** dragons: Enchant-only, 3x base stats, gold material
- **Rainbow** dragons: Breed two shinies, 5x base stats, RGB cycling

## Egg System

### Tiers
| Egg | Price | Best Rarity | Area |
|-----|-------|-------------|------|
| Stone Egg | 100 | Rare | Meadow |
| Crystal Egg | 500 | Epic | Caverns |
| Shadow Egg | 2,500 | Epic | Shadow Realm |
| Golden Egg | 10,000 | Legendary | Sky Temple |
| Mythic Egg | 50,000 | Mythic | Dragon's Peak |
| **Event Egg** | Special | HUGE | Limited time |

### Hatching
- Eggs placed in incubator, timer counts down
- Egg visually glows/cracks as timer progresses (25%, 50%, 75%)
- At 100%: dramatic hatch animation — camera zoom, particles, reveal
- Triple-hatch: hatch 3 eggs at once (gamepass)
- Auto-hatch: eggs hatch without clicking (gamepass)

## Breeding System (Level 8+)
- Place 2 adult dragons on Breeding Altar
- 4-hour breed timer
- Result based on element combo (see fusion chart)
- Small chance of rarity upgrade on breed
- Can discover new fusion elements only through breeding

## Expedition System (Adult+ dragons)
- Send dragons on timed missions (15min, 1hr, 4hr, 8hr)
- Rewards: coins, Dragon Essence, rare food, egg fragments
- Higher dragon stats = better rewards
- Special expeditions unlock with area progression

## Economy

### Currencies
- **Coins** (primary, earned everywhere)
- **Dragon Essence** (premium, from expeditions + daily rewards)
- **Robux** (real money, gamepasses + shop)

### Coin Sinks
- Egg purchases
- Area unlocks
- Plot upgrades
- Enchanting
- Breeding fees

## Monetization (Robux)

### Gamepasses (one-time)
- **Auto-Hatch** (149R) — eggs hatch automatically
- **Triple Hatch** (249R) — hatch 3 eggs at once
- **2x Coins** (99R) — double all coin earnings
- **VIP** (399R) — 2x coins + VIP area + exclusive egg + daily Essence
- **Huge Hunter** (999R) — 2x Huge chance

### Dev Products (repeatable)
- Speed Hatch Potion (25R) — instant hatch 1 egg
- Dragon Essence Pack (50R) — 500 essence
- Lucky Egg (10R) — next egg +1 rarity tier
- Coin Pack (5R-100R) — coin bundles

## Daily Systems
- **Daily Login Rewards** (7-day cycle, resets weekly)
  - Day 1: 500 coins, Day 2: 1000 coins, Day 3: Rare egg
  - Day 4: 2500 coins, Day 5: 50 Essence, Day 6: Epic egg
  - Day 7: Legendary egg + 100 Essence
- **Daily Quests** (3 per day): Hatch X eggs, Feed X dragons, Earn X coins
- **Weekly Quest**: Breed a fusion dragon, Reach area X, etc.

## UI Design
- **Bottom bar**: Inventory, Quests, Shop, Settings (mobile-friendly, big icons)
- **Side HUD**: Coins + Essence counters, XP bar
- **Inventory**: Card grid with dragon portrait, element icon, rarity border, power number
- **Collection Index**: Silhouettes for undiscovered dragons (drives completion)
- **Hatch screen**: Full-screen dramatic reveal with rarity banner

## Polish / Juice
- Screen shake on Legendary+ hatches
- Confetti + fireworks on Mythic/Huge
- Server-wide announcement: "🐉 PlayerName just hatched a HUGE Storm Dragon!"
- Dragon idle behaviors: sleeping, playing, eating, flying short hops
- Weather effects per area (snow in Caverns, storm in Shadow Realm)
- Ambient music per area
- Footstep sounds change by terrain
